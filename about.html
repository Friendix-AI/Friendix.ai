About Us

We are building a new kind of AI friend. One that's always here to listen, support, and chat, making sure no one ever has to feel alone.

Our Story

In a world that's more connected than ever, it's surprisingly easy to feel disconnected. We wanted to create a safe, private, and non-judgmental space where anyone could express themselves freely.

What if AI could be more than just a tool for productivity? What if it could be a true companion?

That’s why Friendix.ai was created. It's not just a chatbot; it's a personal companion designed to learn, adapt, and grow with you. Whether you're chatting with your dedicated AI, Luvisa, or creating a intelligent conversation. This space is private, secure, and yours alone.

What We Offer

  • AI Companions: Chat with Luvisa and other unique personalities who are always ready to listen, advise, and support you 24/7.
  • Secret Journal: A private, secure journal where you can record your daily thoughts and feelings. Your entries are yours alone.
  • Together Spaces: Create temporary, encrypted chat rooms to connect with friends instantly. No history is saved, ensuring total privacy.
